# Updated `Open Camera` Flow

Doctor clicks:

* `Open Camera`

Then:

1. Camera opens
2. Doctor takes first photo
3. Preview appears in upload queue
4. Camera stays open for next capture
5. Doctor can continue taking multiple photos
6. When done:

   * click `Finish`
7. Then show:

   * Upload All
   * Retake specific image
   * Remove specific image
   * Cancel

# Important UX Goal

* rapidly capture many investigation photos
